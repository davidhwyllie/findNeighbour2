The results of EW2 were compared with those from BugMat on a set of 1998 *M. tuberculosis* samples.  
BugMat results are identical to those of EW, except for the filtering steps included in EW2.

The results of all pairwise comparisons are shown in Table 1, below.
It is evident that the results are congruent in all cases, except for a group of samples where EW2 reports 'no match' but Bugmat reports distances < 20.

EW2 by design regards certain poor-quality samples as 'unmatchable'.

Investigations indicate that the samples in the right hand row (reported as "No valid match" by EW2) are because of the filtering criteria used by EW2.
